Accessibility Statement

We want Horizon Synergy websites and products to be usable by as many people as possible.

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

Our Commitment

We aim to improve readability, keyboard access, responsive layouts, clear labels, and helpful contrast across our digital experiences.

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing process. As our products evolve, we will continue improving page structure, alt text, focus states, and compatibility with assistive technologies.

Feedback

If you find an accessibility barrier, email horizonsynergyx@gmail.com with the page URL and a short description of the issue.
