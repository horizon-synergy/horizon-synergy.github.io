Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

At Horizon Synergy, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information across all our games, apps, and digital services, including but not limited to Finly, Vyra, Space Dash, and any future titles.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the app or game, we may collect:

a. Personal Information

  • Email address (for login or communication)
  • Username or nickname
  • Profile/avatar (if applicable)

b. Usage Data

  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Gameplay statistics and logs
  • Crash/error logs
  • Session frequency and duration

c. Advertising ID

Your device's Advertising Identifier (such as Google Ad ID or Apple IDFA), which may be used for analytics or ad personalization.

d. Location Data

Optional and permission-based access to your location (for apps like EcoHive or Shuttle).

2. How We Use Your Data

We may use your data to:

  • Improve app performance and gameplay
  • Save and sync your progress
  • Provide multiplayer matchmaking or lobby features
  • Send optional updates or notifications
  • Monitor performance and crash issues
  • Support customer service

We do not sell your data.

3. Third-Party Services & Their Privacy Policies

Some of our services integrate with third-party platforms. These may collect anonymized or device-level data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Unity Technologies (Analytics & Ads)
  • Firebase by Google
  • Mirror Networking (Open Source)
  • GitHub Pages (if viewing here)

These services are used only to enhance features such as analytics, multiplayer networking, or ads. Data collected by these services is governed by their own privacy policies and may be processed independently of Horizon Synergy. Where possible, you may opt out of personalized ad tracking via your device settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

We store data:

  • Securely, using encryption where needed
  • Only as long as necessary for core functions
  • Locally on your device or on trusted platforms (e.g., Firebase)

5. Your Rights

Depending on your region, such as the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data under privacy laws like the GDPR or CCPA.

However, please note that most data (including Advertising IDs, analytics data, and crash reports) is collected and processed directly by third-party services such as Unity, Google, Yandex, and Firebase. Horizon Synergy does not directly store or control this data, and we cannot fulfill deletion or access requests on their behalf.

We recommend managing your privacy settings directly through your device or by contacting the relevant third-party providers. For example:

  • Unity Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy
  • Yandex Privacy Policy

To limit personalized advertising, you may also update your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking
