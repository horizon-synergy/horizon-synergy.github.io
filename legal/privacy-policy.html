Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters across our apps, games, and digital services.

Effective Date: September 29, 2025

At Horizon Synergy, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information across our games, apps, websites, and digital services, including Space Dash and future titles we develop.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the app, game, website, or service, we may collect the following categories of information.

a. Personal Information

  • Email address for login, support, or communication.
  • Username, nickname, or profile/avatar information where a product offers those features.
  • Messages or details you voluntarily send to us when requesting support.

b. Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Gameplay statistics, logs, session frequency, and session duration.
  • Crash reports, diagnostics, and performance information.

c. Advertising ID

Your device's Advertising Identifier, such as Google Ad ID or Apple IDFA, may be used by advertising or analytics providers for measurement, fraud prevention, or ad personalization where permitted.

d. Location Data

Location access is optional and permission-based. We only request it when it is relevant to a specific product feature.

2. How We Use Your Data

We may use your data to:

  • Improve app performance, gameplay, and product experiences.
  • Save or sync your progress across devices when supported.
  • Provide multiplayer matchmaking, lobby, or community features.
  • Send optional updates, notices, or support responses.
  • Monitor performance, resolve crashes, and protect product integrity.
  • Analyze user behavior in aggregate to improve our products.

We do not sell your data. Your information is used to operate, protect, and improve Horizon Synergy products and services.

3. Third-Party Services & Privacy Policies

Some services may integrate with third-party platforms. These providers may collect anonymized, device-level, or account-level data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Unity Technologies (Analytics & Ads)
  • Firebase by Google
  • Mirror Networking (Open Source)
  • GitHub Pages

Data collected by these services is governed by their own policies and may be processed independently of Horizon Synergy. Where possible, you can opt out of personalized ad tracking through your device settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption: We use appropriate technical protections where needed.
  • Retention: Data is kept only as long as necessary for product, support, legal, or security purposes.
  • Storage: Information may be stored locally on your device or on trusted platforms such as Firebase.
  • Access: Access is limited to authorized personnel and service providers.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal data.

Important note: Some analytics, Advertising ID, and crash report data is collected and processed directly by third-party services such as Unity, Google, and Firebase. Horizon Synergy may not directly control that data and cannot fulfill access or deletion requests on those providers' behalf.

You can manage many privacy choices through your device settings or by contacting the relevant third-party provider. To limit personalized ads, use Android Settings → Google → Ads, or iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate consent. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it as quickly as possible.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or services. When we make changes, we will update the effective date above.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy, contact us at horizonsynergyx@gmail.com.
